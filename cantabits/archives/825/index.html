            I don’t think it will automatically reinstall your apps – all of the apps you have ever downloaded are listed in a special place in (and are free to download from) Google Play (menu button -> “My Apps” -> “All”). However, when you do reinstall an app, your settings and data for that app should be restored if you have “Back up my settings” and “Automatic restore” checked in “Settings” -> “Personal” -> “Backup & reset”. Unfortunately, as you can see from the developer documentation for the backup feature, such functionality is not compulsory and there will certainly be some apps that don’t use it.

            I had thought that this feature would be taken advantage of by the Messages application for backing up SMS/MMS messages, but I don’t think it does as it seems to offer its own “Back up/Restore SMS” option in its menu.

            I guess there is no single relatively simple and guaranteed way of backing up and restoring everything on Android. 🙁
